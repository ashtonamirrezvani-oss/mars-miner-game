# 🚀 Mars Miner: Escape to Earth

A thrilling mobile web game! You are an astronaut miner on the red planet. Tap to mine valuable resources, build your survival base, upgrade your equipment, and eventually construct an escape ship to return home to Earth.

## How to Play

1. **Mine Resources**: Tap anywhere on the Mars surface canvas to drill. You'll collect Regolith (common building material), Water Ice, Iron Ore, and rare Helium-3.
2. **Upgrade Your Gear**: Use resources to level up your Mining Laser (more yield per tap) and deploy Mining Drones for passive income.
3. **Build Your Base**: Construct Solar Arrays (energy), Habitat Modules (survival), Mining Rigs (passive regolith), Refineries (better yields), and finally the Launch Pad.
4. **Escape to Earth**: Gather enough resources to build your Mars Escape Ship. When ready, launch and win!

## Features
- Fully responsive mobile design with large touch targets
- Real-time canvas animation with particle effects
- Automatic saving to browser (localStorage)
- Idle progression with passive mining
- Exciting win screen with confetti
